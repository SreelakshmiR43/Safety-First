Message Of District Police Chief, Kottayam

SRI. KARTHICK IPS DISTRICT POLICE CHIEF, KOTTAYAM,KERALA
I convey my heartiest greetings and best wishes to the people of the Kottayam District and all the members of Kottayam District Police, on assuming charge as District Police Chief. The primary task of the police force is to maintain law & ampamp order and also prevent and detect crime. It does not stop there. It is my duty and responsibility to ensure the all of you feel safe, especially the poor/under privileged and downtrodden. We are here to serve you and protect you. We have built a web interface for the public through this website, so as to enable the public to know more about the Kottayan District Police. We are in the process of becoming an active participant in the social networking sites like Twitter, Facebook etc also so as to more accessible to the citizens. I expect your wholehearted cooperation and also reiterate our commitment to serve the people sincerely, effectively and without any corruption
PINARAYI VIJAYAN
CHIEF MINISTER,KERALA

Message Of Hon'ble Chief Minister

I am extremely delighted that, Kerala Police is switching to the new CMS Platform for its Website. I am proud that, the Service Delivery System of Kerala Police has received appreciation from the people and this website will contain information, which will help the Public to ask for legitimate services from the Police. I wish the Police and the Visitors to the Website all the very best.

ANIL KANT IPS
DGP & STATE POLICE CHIEF,KERALA

My greetings to all of you. It is my honor and privilege to be part of the community and serve as the State Police Chief of this beautiful State. People expect that the Police Officers treat them with respect, behave decently and uphold justice. All members of the Police Force should strive hard to meet these expectations.
